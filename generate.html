My Generate script is the engine behind this website. It creates everything based on templates.
It's not the prettiest layout or the prettiest hack, but it works for me.

The first thing you need to do is to find a suitable layout template for your site. I googled free website templates and a lot of sites showed up.

The site is composed of 3 parts:
  1. The header
  2. The body or the content
  3. The footer
